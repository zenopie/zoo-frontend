RULES

House Edge 0.131%

The goal of blackjack is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21.

Face cards are worth 10. Aces are worth 1 or 11, whichever makes a better hand.

If the dealer's face up card is 10 or an ace the dealer will check for blackjack.

Insurance is a bet equal to half your original bet and pays out 2 to 1 if the dealer has blackjack

A dealer blackjack wins all hands except against a player blackjack

If you are dealt 21 from the start (Ace & 10), you got a blackjack

Blackjack pays out 3/2 plus your original bet

A push result means the player and the dealer tie and the player gets back their original bet

A win result means the player gets back their bet, plus an amount equal to their bet

To 'Hit' is to ask for another card. To 'Stand' is to hold your total and end your turn.

If you go over 21 you bust, and the dealer wins regardless of the dealer's hand.

Dealer will hit until his/her cards total 17 or higher.

Doubling is like a hit, only the bet is doubled and you only get one more card.

You can only Double Down on a deal of 10 or 11

Split can be done when you have two of the same card - the pair is split into two hands.

Splitting also doubles the bet, because each new hand is worth the original bet.

Surrendering gives up your hand and pays back half your bet

You can only double/split/surrender on the first move
